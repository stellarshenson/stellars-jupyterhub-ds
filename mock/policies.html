Policies

The nine policy types - what each group can grant, and how they combine
{ic:settings}Environment
Inject env vars. Priority-first-wins, reserved names rejected.
{ic:cpu}GPU
Grant all or specific device ids. OR-grant, union, all-wins.
{ic:server}Docker
Raw socket or limited quotas + privileged. Raw supersedes limited.
{ic:cpu}CPU
Core limit. Biggest enabled value wins.
{ic:cpu}Memory
GB limit + swap toggle. Biggest enabled value wins.
{ic:shield}Sudo
Section-gated. Highest-priority active group wins.
{ic:shield}Downloads
Block/allow file downloads. Highest-priority active wins.
{ic:key}API keys
Credential pool, one slot per user, collision-free over restarts.
{ic:server}Volume mounts
Mount shared volumes. Union by mountpoint, protected paths barred.
Effective policy preview
Pick a user to see their resolved policy across all groups (the spawn-time effective set), and impersonate "view as" to preview their access - the server owns the summary, the client just renders it.
